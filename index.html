// How it works

3 AI models in synergy

Designed to assist analysis through multiple complementary signals

TensorFlow.js

Deep learning running 100% locally in your browser. Analyzes image semantics and detects statistical patterns common in AI-generated content.

⚡ 100% local & private

LLaVA via Cloudflare

Multimodal LLaVA-1.5 model hosted on Cloudflare Workers AI. Understands image content and provides a probabilistic assessment regarding AI generation with reasoning.

🌐 Pro infrastructure

Pixel Analysis

Proprietary algorithm that examines JPEG compression artifacts, digital noise, generation patterns, and abnormal smoothing signatures.

🔍 Pattern estimation

Video Forensics

4-layer probabilistic pipeline for short-form video — inter-frame noise coherence, local temporal consistency (2-3s window), audio TTS detection, and LLaVA+LLaMA vision. Returns an ai_probability score, a confidence level, and a list of detected anomalies. Never a binary verdict.

// About

A transparent technology

Our mission: make AI image probability analysis accessible to assist human judgment

Privacy first

Your images are never stored on our servers. The TensorFlow.js analysis runs entirely in your browser.

Transparency over certainty

This tool highlights patterns and anomalies to help users make informed decisions, supporting human-assisted evaluation rather than absolute claims.

Cloudflare infrastructure

Global edge hosting, minimal latency, high availability, and 10,000 free requests per day.

Probabilistic by design

Every result — image or video — is expressed as a probability (0–100 %), a confidence level (low / medium / high), and a nuanced verdict. The system never claims certainty: it surfaces signals to assist your judgment, not replace it.

Important Notice: Analytical Assistance Tool

This tool is designed to provide probabilistic signals and an informative opinion on the analyzed images and videos. The Artificial Intelligence models used (TensorFlow, LLaVA, etc.) can make mistakes (false positives or false negatives) and have limitations. The results displayed do not constitute absolute proof. Human judgment remains strictly necessary to determine the true nature of a file.
